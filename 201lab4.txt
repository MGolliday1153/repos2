Troubleshooting a problem
1. Identify the problem
2. Establish a theory
3. Test the theory
4. Evaluate (does it work yes or no)
		a. No- Establish a new theory and test new theory
		b. Yes- Establish a plan of action, implement a plan, verify full system functionality,
			  document findings, IT WORKS!
With these steps we can figure out how to solve any troubleshooting issues.
